About me

I am a second year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering department at the University of Wisconsin-Madison advised by Prof. Ramya K. Vinayak and Prof. Robert D. Nowak. My research interests lie in the intersection of machine learning, statistics and signal processing.

I received the B.S. degrees in Electrical & Electronics Engineering and in Mathematics, and M.Sc. degree in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from Koc University, where I was advised by Prof. Alper Erdogan.
